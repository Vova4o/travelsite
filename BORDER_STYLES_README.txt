==========================================================
BORDER STYLES - HOW TO SWITCH BETWEEN OPTIONS
==========================================================

Currently Active: OPTION 1 (Subtle Shadow & Border)

To switch to a different style, find these 4 lines in index.html
and replace "option1-borders" with your choice:

Line ~1895: class="page-section option1-borders
Line ~2363: class="page-section option1-borders  (change this line)
Line ~2777: class="page-section option1-borders  (change this line)
Line ~3187: class="page-section option1-borders  (change this line)

==========================================================
AVAILABLE OPTIONS:
==========================================================

option1-borders - Subtle Shadow & Border (RECOMMENDED)
  • Soft shadow around each block
  • Light gold border for definition
  • Clean and modern look
  • Best for professional appearance

option2-borders - Gold Accent Borders
  • Thicker gold borders (3px)
  • More prominent separation
  • Elegant and luxurious
  • Best for high-end brand feel

option3-borders - Card Style with Hover Effect
  • Card-like appearance
  • Interactive hover effect (lifts on hover)
  • Modern and engaging
  • Best for interactive feel

==========================================================
EASY FIND & REPLACE:
==========================================================

To switch ALL sections at once, use find & replace:
  Find:    option1-borders
  Replace: option2-borders  (or option3-borders)

This will update all 4 sections simultaneously.
